# Baked Apples
---
## Ingredients
- 4 Green Apples
  - _Medium_ Sized
- â…“ Cup Brown Sugar
- Handful of Raisins
- 1 Tablespoon Apple Pie Spice
- Spoon Coconut Oil
  - One spoon per apple
- Dash of Salt
- Dash of Apple Cider
  - Mixed with water

## Instructions
1. Core the apples and put them in a deep dish (_that has a lid_).
2. Stuff the middle of the apples with raisins.
3. Add the brown sugar and apple pie spice to the top.
4. Put a small dollop of coconut oil on top of each apple and sprinkle with a little salt.
5. Put the water / apple cider in the bottom (about 3 Tablespoons of mixture).
6. Cover with the lid and microwave for 5 minutes and check on the apples
7. Microwave another 3-5 minutes and watch for the inside of the apple to overflow
