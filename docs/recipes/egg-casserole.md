# Egg Casserole
---
## Ingredients
- 12 Eggs
- 16 Oz. Cottage Cheese
  - Small Curd
- Shredded Hash Browns
- Milk
- Shredded Cheese
  - Shredded
- 1 Bell Pepper
- 1 White Onion
- Spinach
- Turkey Bacon
  - Optional, pulled into small pieces
- 21 Seasoning Salute
- Salt
- Pepper

## Instructions
1. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl.
2. Pour into greased 9x13 pan. Sprinkle shredded cheese on top.
