# Grilled Zucchini & Squash
---
## Ingredients
- Zucchini
- Squash
- Olive Oil Spray
- 21 Seasoning Salute
- Salt
- Pepper

## Instructions
Preheat oven to 425Â°F.

1. Put parchment paper on cooking sheet. Cut zucchini & squash into small pieces.
2. Spray olive oil on the vegetables. Add salt, pepper, and 21 seasoning salute.

Bake for 20 minutes.
